ABOUT

ON.THE.WAY is a tracker app that registers the emotions experienced by the user.
With ON.THE.WAY you are able to get in touch with yourself and understand your feelings and how to manage them.
The app promotes a healthy life with a focus on the importance of mental health.

ON.THE.WAY was designed and created by a group of 4 students, Elisa Sampaio, Luana Barbosa, Márcia Lopes and Vera Vasconcelos.
